# Writing Mock 09 - Example Answers

## TASK 1.1 (Informal Note) - ~50 words

**Task:** Write a letter to your friend, who is also a student at this school. Write about your feelings and what you think the magazine management should do about the situation.

### Example Answer:

Hi Sarah,

I'm really excited about the new Student Voices section! I think it's a great idea. Maybe we could write about student life and challenges. I'd love to contribute something about managing school stress. I think the magazine should ask more students for their opinions too.

Let me know what you think!

Best,
Alex

---

## TASK 1.2 (Formal Letter) - 120-150 words

**Task:** Write a letter to the manager. Write about your feelings and what you think the magazine management should do about the situation.

### Example Answer:

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to express my enthusiastic support for the new Student Voices section in the school magazine. I believe this initiative will greatly benefit our school community by encouraging diverse opinions and fostering dialogue among students.

I would like to suggest several improvements. Firstly, the magazine management should actively promote the section through social media and class announcements to increase participation. Secondly, I recommend featuring student interviews alongside written articles to make the content more engaging and accessible.

Additionally, I would be honored to contribute an article about effective study techniques for examinations. I am confident that many students would find this topic valuable and relevant.

Thank you for considering my suggestions. I look forward to seeing this exciting new section flourish.

Yours sincerely,
Michael Johnson

## TASK 2 (Blog Post) - 180-200 words

**Task:** You are writing a blog post about the advantages and disadvantages of working from home. Explain your opinion and give examples to support your ideas.

### Example Answer:

## Working from Home: Benefits and Drawbacks

Working from home has become increasingly popular in recent years, offering both significant advantages and notable disadvantages that deserve careful consideration.

**Advantages:**

The primary benefit of remote work is flexibility. Employees can create their own schedules and work environment, leading to increased productivity. For example, a software developer can work during their most focused hours without office distractions. Additionally, working from home eliminates commuting time and expenses, allowing professionals to save money and enjoy a better work-life balance. Parents particularly appreciate this arrangement as it enables them to manage family responsibilities more effectively.

**Disadvantages:**

However, remote work presents challenges. Social isolation can affect mental health, as employees miss face-to-face interaction with colleagues. Communication can become difficult, leading to misunderstandings. Furthermore, maintaining work-life boundaries proves challenging when your office is your bedroom. Technical issues and internet problems can disrupt productivity unexpectedly.

**Conclusion:**

In my opinion, working from home works best as a hybrid arrangement. Combining remote days with occasional office visits balances flexibility with social connection. Ultimately, the success of remote work depends on individual preferences, job type, and company culture.
